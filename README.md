## What is LanScan?  

**LanScan** is a powerful yet easy-to-use **local network scanner for macOS** that quickly identifies every device connected to your home or office LAN. Whether you‚Äôre optimizing Wi-Fi performance, checking for unauthorized devices, or mapping your subnet, LanScan provides accurate and detailed results in seconds.  

This utility automatically detects your active network interfaces, scans for reachable hosts, and lists their **IP addresses, MAC addresses, hostnames, and vendor information**. The result is a clear and structured view of your network, helping you monitor connectivity and troubleshoot network-related problems efficiently.  

LanScan is suitable for both beginners and professionals. IT admins can use it for diagnostic audits, while home users benefit from its simplicity and speed. It supports scanning across multiple interfaces and subnets, displays real-time updates as devices connect or disconnect, and offers **custom range scans** for advanced users.  

The app runs natively on **Apple Silicon (M1, M2, M3)** for maximum speed and accuracy, using minimal system resources. LanScan is a must-have utility for anyone who needs to keep an eye on their network ‚Äî secure, fast, and straightforward.
